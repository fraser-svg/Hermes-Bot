# Example Emails — Retargeting Cold Outreach

Reference samples. Adapt the structure, do not copy verbatim.

---

## Sample 1 — Edinburgh Electrician (fb_pixel_leak)

**To:** info@brightspark-edi.co.uk
**Subject:** Emergency electrician ad — quick observation

```
Hi Mark,

Saw your "24/7 Emergency Electrician Edinburgh" ad on Facebook
yesterday — caught my eye because brightspark-edi.co.uk doesn't
have a Facebook Pixel installed.

Means every click you're paying for is one-shot. Can't retarget,
can't build lookalike audiences, can't tell Meta which clicks
became calls. The algorithm is optimizing for clicks, not jobs.

30-min fix. Reply "audit" and I'll send a 5-min Loom showing
exactly what's missing and where it goes.

Fraser
```

**Why it works:** quotes exact ad, names exact missing pixel, single soft CTA, no links, no fluff.

## Sample 2 — Glasgow Plumber (multi_platform_leak)

**Subject:** Glasgow plumber — 2 ad leaks

```
Hi Aiden,

Looked at glasgowplumbers24.com after seeing both your "Boiler
Repair Glasgow" Meta ad and a Google search ad for "emergency
plumber Glasgow".

Two things: no Facebook Pixel, and no Google Ads remarketing
tag. So neither platform can show your ads to people who already
visited but didn't call. With your ad volume that's likely
hundreds of warm leads slipping through every month.

Both fixes take under an hour combined. Reply "leaks" and I'll
send a short Loom for each.

Fraser
```

**Subject:** LinkedIn Insight Tag missing on northsec.co.uk

```
For B2B that breaks the whole pipeline — you can't see which

Worth 10 minutes? Reply "insight" and I'll send a Loom for your

Fraser
```

## Reply Patterns

If they reply "audit" / "tag" / "leaks" / "insight":
- Send the promised Loom (record actual screen of their site, not generic).
- Include 1 concrete next step ("If you want, I can install it for £150 or send you the snippet free").

Never auto-reply with a generic deck. Lose the trust immediately.
