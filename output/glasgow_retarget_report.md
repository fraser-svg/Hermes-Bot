## Methodology

1. **Discovery** — `prospect_no_pixel.py` searched Google Maps for each of 13 categories in Glasgow, scraped each homepage and ran HTML pattern matching for Facebook Pixel, Google Ads remarketing tag, LinkedIn Insight Tag, TikTok Pixel, GTM, GA.
2. **Meta verification** — Headless Chromium scraped `facebook.com/ads/library/?q=NAME&country=GB&active_status=active` and parsed the active result count.
3. **Google Ads verification** — Headless Chromium scraped `adstransparency.google.com/?region=GB&domain=DOMAIN` and counted `<creative-preview>` elements.
4. **Qualification** — A lead qualifies if `(meta_has_ads AND no fb_pixel) OR (gatc_has_ads AND no google_ads_remarketing)`.

## Caveats

- **Meta name collision** — Searches by name can match national chains. Tier 3 needs manual verification; Tier 4 (>1000 matches) excluded as noise.
- **GATC by domain** — More precise than Meta (uses the actual website domain, not name).
- **Pixel scan = homepage only** — Pixels hidden on /quote or /contact would be missed (~5% false-positive expected).
- **Active ads only (Meta)** — Paused ads in last 90 days won't show.
- **LinkedIn Ads not checked** — Requires `LINKEDIN_COOKIE` env var.
